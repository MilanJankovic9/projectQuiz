  • INTRO

    Interprefy Computer-based learning system is designed to provide interactive and hands on experience of our remote interpretation platform. Through numerous videos and practical tests you can learn how to use the platform and prepare for future events. This test will take you through the most important functions on the platform that are necessary for delivering a perfect event. Please have in mind that you will have only one attempt to complete the test and only one attempt per question. This test requires using a laptop for completion and it requires certain parts of your keyboard to answer some of the questions. Once you are ready, select start and begin the test.

    We wish you good luck!

  • Prerequisites for a successful event

    This section is about hardware requirements for a successful event.

    • Minimum 2Mbps download and upload per
  language channel
• 8Mbps download is recommended for HD
  quality or if multiple video channels
  are received
• Laptops: Core i5, 4GB RAM, windows 7 or
  higher;
• Recommended CPU benchmark is above 3500.
• USB headset with noise cancelling, to
  prevent the sound from the ear pad being
  picked up by the microphone –
• Sennheiser SC70USB (also called Sennheiser
  506504 or 506502) is our recommendation
  for best quality headset
• Professional USB microphone - Blue Yeti
• Ethernet cable connection is an absolute
  must!

About Our Company

Interprefy is a Swiss technology company that provides a cloud-based software platform for remote simultaneous interpreting for meetings, conferences and events.

Headquarters are located in Zurich, Switzerland. In addition, Interprefy has an office in Chicago, USA, which caters to clients in Northern and Southern America and recently Japan.
