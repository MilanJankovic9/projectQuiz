  • Prerequisites for successful event

    This section is about hardware requirements for a successful event.

    • Minimum 2Mbps download and upload per language channel • 8Mbps download is recommended for HD quality or if multiple video channels are received • Laptops: Core i5, 4GB RAM, windows 7 or higher; • Recommended CPU benchmark is above 3500. • USB headset with noise cancelling, to prevent the sound from the ear pad being picked up by the microphone – • Sennheiser SC70USB (also called Sennheiser 506504 or 506502) is our recommendation for best quality headset • Sennheiser SC60USB, SC260USB and Jabra Evolve 40 are good alternatives • Logitech H340 is recommended on a budget • Arama is recommended model for Asian market • No Bluetooth headsets are allowed. • Alternatively a professional USB microphone – Audio Technica AT2020USB, Blue Snowball and Blue Yeti are the preferred choices. • Optional: professional interpreting panel, laptop connected to on-site PA system • Ethernet cable connection is an absolute must!

About Our Company

Interprefy is a Swiss technology company that provides a cloud-based software platform for remote simultaneous interpreting for meetings, conferences and events.

Headquarters are located in Zurich, Switzerland. In addition, Interprefy has an office in Chicago, USA, which caters to clients in Northern and Southern America and recently Japan.
